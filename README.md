# Gradely — Need to Know Guide 🏥

This guide explains every section of the Gradely app in simple, student-friendly language.

---

## 1. What is Gradely?

Gradely is a free GPA calculator built specifically for students of Dunkwa On Offin Nursing and Midwifery Training College (DONMTC).

Just like a nurse checks a patient's vital signs to know their health status — Gradely checks your academic vital signs so you always know where you stand.

No manual calculations. No guessing. No surprises at the end of the year. Just enter your grades and the app does everything else for you.

It was built by a DONMTC student, for DONMTC students. It is completely free and 100% anonymous — no one can see your data, not even the SRC.

---

## 2. The Calculator Tab (🧮)

This is where you start. The Calculator tab is your main workspace for entering grades.

### HOW IT WORKS:

- **Select your year** at the top (Year 1, Year 2, or Year 3)
- **All your courses load automatically** — already preloaded with the official DONMTC curriculum
- **Select your grade** for each course, and your GPA calculates instantly.

### SAVING YOUR RESULTS:

- Once you have entered all your grades, tap **'Save Semester 1'** or **'Save Semester 2'**.
- Saved results flow into your Cumulative GPA automatically.

### RESIT GRADES:

- If you resitted a course, tap **'Add Resit'** below that course.
- Enter both your original grade and your resit grade.
- The app automatically averages them as per DONMTC academic policy.

---

## 3. The Cumulative Tab (📊)

The Cumulative tab is your academic dashboard. It shows your big picture.

### WHAT IS CUMULATIVE GPA?

Your Cumulative GPA (CGPA) is the overall average of all your grades across all years and semesters. This determines your final degree classification.

### DEGREE CLASSIFICATIONS:

- **3.60 - 4.00** = FIRST CLASS 🥇
- **3.00 - 3.59** = SECOND CLASS UPPER
- **2.50 - 2.99** = SECOND CLASS LOWER
- **2.00 - 2.49** = THIRD CLASS
- **1.00 - 1.99** = PASS
- **Below 1.00** = FAIL

---

## 4. The Projection Tab (🎯)

The Projection tab is your planning tool. Use it before exams to simulate different grade scenarios.

### WHAT-IF SIMULATOR:

Test two different grade scenarios side by side:

- **Scenario A:** Your current plan.
- **Scenario B:** What if you did better?
  Compare them to see how your CGPA changes.

### WHAT GRADE DO I NEED?

Select your target classification, and the app calculates the minimum average grade point you need across your remaining courses to hit that target.

---

## 5. The Profile Tab (👤)

Your personal hub for managing account settings and app preferences.

- **Edit Profile:** Update nickname or programme.
- **Install App:** Instructions for adding Gradely to your home screen.
- **Privacy & Data:** Explain how your data stays on your device.
- **Reset Data:** Permanently delete all saved GPA records.

---

## 6. Your Privacy (🔒)

- **What we collect:** Nothing. Absolutely nothing is sent anywhere.
- **Where your data lives:** Only on your phone or browser storage.
- **Who can see your data:** Only you. Not the SRC, the college, or the developer.

---

## 7. Understanding Your GPA (📐)

Calculated using the official DONMTC grade point scale:

- A = 4.00, A- = 3.75, B+ = 3.50, B = 3.00, B- = 2.50, C+ = 2.00, C = 1.50, D = 1.00, F = 0.00.
  Semester GPA = Total Grade Points ÷ Total Credits.
  CGPA = Total Points (all semesters) ÷ Total Credits (all semesters).
