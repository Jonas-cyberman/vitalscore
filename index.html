Need to Know Guide 🏥

1. What is Gradely?

Gradely is a free GPA calculator built specifically for students of Dunkwa On Offin Nursing and Midwifery Training College (DNMTC).

Just like a nurse checks a patient's vital signs — Gradely checks your academic vital signs so you always know where you stand.

2. How it Works

  • 100% Private: Your data never leaves your phone. We don't use accounts or index numbers.
  • Official Curriculum: Courses and credit hours are pre-loaded based on your programme.
  • Real-time Math: As soon as you select a grade, your GPA is updated.

3. Classifications

  • 3.60 - 4.00: First Class 🥇
  • 3.00 - 3.59: Second Class Upper
  • 2.50 - 2.99: Second Class Lower
  • 2.00 - 2.49: Third Class
  • 1.00 - 1.99: Pass
  • Below 1.00: Fail

4. Pro Tips 💡

• Save your results in the Calculator tab to see them in Cumulative.

• Use the Projection tab before exams to plan your study goals.

• Add Gradely to your Home Screen for faster offline access.
